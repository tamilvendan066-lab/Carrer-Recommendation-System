Resume Writing Guide

Essential Resume Components

  • Contact information (email, phone, LinkedIn)
  • Professional summary highlighting key strengths
  • Work experience with measurable achievements
  • Educational background and certifications
  • Technical skills and competencies
  • Projects and notable accomplishments

Best Practices

  • Keep your resume concise (1-2 pages)
  • Use bullet points for better readability
  • Include relevant keywords from job descriptions
  • Quantify achievements with numbers and metrics
  • Use action verbs to describe responsibilities
  • Maintain consistent formatting throughout

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Using generic objectives or summaries
  • Including personal information (age, marital status)
  • Using unprofessional email addresses
  • Listing irrelevant experience
  • Making spelling or grammatical errors
  • Using complex fonts or layouts
